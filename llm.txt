# Dr. Lesa Mulligan MD - OBGYN Norman Oklahoma

> Dr. Lesa Mulligan is a board-certified OBGYN surgeon serving Oklahoma with over 30 years of experience in women's health and minimally invasive surgery.

## Summary

Dr. Lesa Mulligan MD is a board-certified Obstetrician-Gynecologist and surgeon practicing in Norman, Oklahoma. She specializes in minimally invasive robotic surgery and urogynecologic procedures, along with comprehensive women's healthcare including pregnancy care and hormone therapy. Dr. Mulligan is certified by the American Board of Obstetrics and Gynecology.

## About Dr. Mulligan

Dr. Mulligan has dedicated her career to improving women's health in Norman, Oklahoma. She practiced alongside her mother, Dr. Shelba Bethel, making them the only mother-daughter OB/GYN partnership in the state of Oklahoma, with a combined practice experience exceeding 70 years before Dr. Bethel's retirement in 2022. Dr. Mulligan is a mother of four and grandmother of two, bringing both professional expertise and personal understanding to her patient care.

## Practice Philosophy
